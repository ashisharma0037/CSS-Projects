Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all the objects that move around it. There are eight planets including Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Earth is the only known planet where life exists.

Galaxy

A galaxy is a huge collection of stars, gas, dust, and planets held together by gravity. Our galaxy is called the Milky Way. Scientists believe there are billions of galaxies in the universe.

Black Hole

A black hole is a region in space where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape from it.

Nebula

A nebula is a cloud of gas and dust in space. They are the birthplaces of stars.
